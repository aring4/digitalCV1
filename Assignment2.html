Get To Know Alex Ring

Introduction

Alex Ring is a sophmore student at Elon University studying Communication Design and minoring in Art. Alex Ring hopes to work in the Fashion Industry when she graduates.

Personal life

Alex Ring is from Scarsdale, New York. Alex grew up creating art, whether it was painting, sketching, drawing or sculpting. She enjoys cooking various different cuisines especially pasta with vodka sauce which is her favorite meal to cook as well as eat! Alex has a passion for fashion and worked as a sales associate at Reformation last summer in Noho, NYC.
